The problem

Everyone is working hard.
But the work is fragmented.

It's 9am and the day is already moving. Everyone is capable — but context lives in corridors, messages get passed through people, and too much is held in someone's head.

📞
Messages get lost
A client calls for an update. The vet is in consult. The message is written down, passed on — or held until someone can catch them at the right moment.
🗒️
Nothing lands
A new protocol sits on a noticeboard, hoping people read it. SOPs are in folders, out of date. Change is hard to make stick when there's no shared place for it to live.
🔧
Small things stay broken
The autoclave handle is still loose. People notice and mention it — five times. Nothing quite lands because there's no shared place for things to go.
📋
Insurance costs hours
Manual entry into Vet Envoy. 5–15 minutes per claim. Chasing three people for one form. £100s a week in admin time that shouldn't be admin at all.
🌙
Handovers fall apart
End of day, cases are updated in people's heads. A colleague picking up tomorrow — or a locum — doesn't have the full picture. Context gets lost between shifts.
👁️
Good work goes unseen
Nurses, receptionists, prep staff — brilliant work that holds a practice together. But there's no channel for it. Recognition happens by accident, not by design.
The solution

Now look at the same day with Helm.

Everything has a place. The right people are looped in without interrupting consult. Tasks are owned. Handovers are clear — and the team leaves differently.

💬
Structured threads
Loop in the right vet without interrupting consult. Clinical context pulled in automatically.
✅
Tasks with ownership
Assign anything to anyone. Follow-ups, equipment issues, calls — logged and tracked, not forgotten.
⚡
Insurance in a click
AI reads clinical documents, fills the claim, flags what's missing. 15 minutes becomes under one.
🔍
Live SOPs & search
SOPs are searchable and used every day. Ask a question, get the answer immediately.
📊
Full visibility
The practice manager sees what's been done, what hasn't, and where support is needed.
🏥
Works with your PMS
Helm doesn't replace your clinical system. It sits around it and makes everything else work properly.
